About Us.

We are BrainBox.

We are a team of 6 students from Heriot-Watt, and our mission is to create and provide a service that can help groups effectively communicate and collaborate - wherever they are.

We are experienced in collaboration and in the use of networking apps and have made our own product to provide you with the powerful tools that other services don't, to help your team put their minds together and take the hassle out of managing your communication apps.

For more information about our product, please visit our home page..
